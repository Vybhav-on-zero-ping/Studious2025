About Student Chat

Primary

Student Chat is a messaging platform built by students, for students. Whether you're working on group projects, need help with homework, or just want to connect with classmates, Student Chat is your go-to space.

🎓 What You Can Do

  • 📚 Ask and answer academic questions
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Collaborate with classmates in group chats
  • 📝 Share notes, resources, and updates
  • 📅 Stay organized with study groups and planning tools

💡 Why We Built It

We believe communication is the key to better learning. Our mission is to make it easier for students to support each other and succeed together — no matter the subject, grade level, or timezone.

🔒 Safety First

All messages are private to your groups. We also have moderators and simple reporting tools to help keep the platform respectful and distraction-free.

🚀 Join the Community

Student Chat is more than an app — it’s a growing community of learners helping each other grow. Jump in, start a conversation, and be part of something smarter.
